Problem 9.   A soft-drink bottling company fills and ships soda in plastic bottles with a target volume of 354 milliliters. The filling machinery does not deliver a perfectly consistent volume of liquid to each bottle, and the three quartiles for the fill volume are Q1 = 351, Q2 = 357, and Q3 = 360.

Find the IQR.


A) 13.5
B) 10.8
C) 3
D) 9


View Answer

Ans : D

Explanation: IQR = Q3 - Q1 = 360 - 351 = 9.
